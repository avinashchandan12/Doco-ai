# 🌾 The Problem

Rural doctors often work with:

• Limited specialist access\
• Diagnostic uncertainty\
• Prescription safety risks\
• Paper-based records\
• Delayed escalation of high-risk cases

👉 **Result: A Clinical Decision Support Gap**

Not a shortage of doctors ---\
but a shortage of intelligent assistance at the point of care.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 🤖 The Solution --- Doco Assist AI

Doco Assist AI is a **doctor-specialised Clinical Decision Support System
(CDSS)**.

✔ AI Clinical Assistant\
✔ Prescription Safety Validation\
✔ Red-Flag Detection\
✔ Structured Clinical Insights\
✔ Rural-Optimized Design

👉 **Decision Support, Not Diagnosis**

AI assists. Doctors decide.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 🚀 Key Capabilities

• **AI Clinical Co-Pilot**\
Structured reasoning from symptoms & vitals

• **Prescription Intelligence**\
